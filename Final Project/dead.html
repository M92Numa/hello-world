About The Deadbeatz

For fans of Stevie Ray Vaughan, Black Keys, Jon Spencer Blues Explosion, & The Allman Brothers. The Deadbeatz are a spacey garage rock jam band out of Milford, CT. Bringing a variety of blues, grunge, and rock to every venue they play at, The Deadbeats are one of the jam bands on the local scene to be reckoned with.
