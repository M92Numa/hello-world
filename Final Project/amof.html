About A Matter of Fact

For fans of Soundgarden, Incubus, 10 Years, & The Stooges. A Matter of Fact brings an eclectic taste having taken inspiration & influence from genres all across the board. Serving as the brain child of musician Matt Numa, all music fans are sure to find something out of A Matter of Fact.
