About Glenn VeryClose

For fans of Tool, My Bloody Valentine, Failure, & Modest Mouse. Glenn VeryClose started out as the solo project for The 30th of February frontman Anthony Pieger. Eventually he enlisted the help of Dead Beatz frontman Spencer Schermerhorn & Thejudasobscure brain child Ryan Woody on bass & drums respectively. Since then, the supergroup has brought heavy punk & shoegaze to each and every venue they play.
